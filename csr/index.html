Ecom Express organised a blood donation campaign in collaboration with Red Cross Society in June 2015. Organized as part of the Company’s Corporate Social Responsibility programme, employees from Ecom Express rolled up their sleeves to bleed for a good cause. More than 350 employees participated in the drive.

2016

With a firm belief that quality education, as a right for all, must work hand in hand with a healthy environment, Ecom Express launched ‘Rainbow - Spreading colours of joy’ initiative. As part of the initiative, Ecom Express renovated the Government Primary School in Sarai (Alawardi) in Gurgaon, one of the most underprivileged schools in the area. The activities included plastering, flooring, and painting of the school building that houses 250 students. Ecom Express also provided chairs and tables for the teachers and sports items for the school children.

Ecom Express takes pride to be amongst the trusted partners of Alibaba Group on Mission Million Books Campaign launched by the Grouping association with Crosswords and Ratna Nidhi Charitable Trust. Aimed at distributing cost-free reading material to educational institutions in India,the mission is driven by the noble spirit to have many confident young citizens stand up in the society. Within a short span of 10 days itself, Ecom Express contributed over 16000 educational books donated by its employees. As the trusted logistics partner, the Company has further committed a contribution of USD 500 per month towards the successful execution of the Campaign.

Road safety is important for Ecom Express, and the Company is always looking at ways to increase awareness of all road users. This year, to celebrate National Road Safety Week 2017 from January 9 -15, Ecom Express partnered with the Traffic Police Department of various states to help spread awareness about road safety. By engaging with the community to help change unsafe behaviour on the roads with simple but effective messages, Ecom Express demonstrated how it recognises unsafe driving/riding bikes as the biggest risks to the safety of its employees and members of the public, and shows its on-going commitment to reducing accidents.
