Managing cross-border transport for elite global auto race

Agility Fairs & Events is the trusted transporter for the high-value racecars competing in an annual, globally recognized racing competition. Moving the cars across borders requires organization of shipping, transporting and handling. Special attention is devoted not only to the cars but also to the areas where they are unloaded. Agility Fairs & Events obtains specialized customs documentation and clearance requirements at the race venue and coordinates road closures so that the cars, their spare parts and tires can be unloaded safely. Once the race concludes, Agility repacks the cars, parts and tires in their specialized containers and has them cleared from public roads. All of this activity takes place within a tight timeline and with strict adherence to health and safety specifications.
