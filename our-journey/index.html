OUR JOURNEY

OUR JOURNEY

2012

2012

August 1, 2012

Incorporated as a Private Limited company

2013

2013

January 1, 2013

  • Started operations on 31 January, 2013 in 35 cities with a team size of 300+ and 42 Delivery centres
  • Launched operations in the state of Jammu and Kashmir
  • Started operations in the Western and Southern regions of India
  • Expanded to 100+ Delivery Centres
2014

2014

March 1, 2014

Expanded operations to the cities of Hyderabad, Chennai, Kanyakumari and Pune

Raised Series A investment of USD 17 Million from Peepul Capital

Increased team size to over 1300 staff

Expanded to 300+ Delivery Centers

2015

2015

January 1, 2015

  • Established the first country-wide express ground transportation network to enhance consistency and reduce dependence on air.
  • Raised Series B investment of USD 133 Mn from Warburg Pincus
  • Launched operations in Eastern India, thereby expanding footprint to All India
  • Completed full-state coverage of Delhi, Haryana and Goa
  • Increased team size to over 7400 staff
  • Expanded to 650+ Delivery Centers
2016

2016

January 1, 2016

  • Expanded full-state coverage to Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka
  • Expanded services to the remotest parts of the North-East region including Aizawl and Dimapur
  • Launched INDIA-1, a state of the art facility spread across 500,000 sq.ft. housing a Pickup Processing Centre, a Hub and Fulfillment Centre, in Bilaspur (Haryana)
  • Increased team size to over 14000 staff with presence in near 1000 towns and 1200 Delivery Centres
2017

2017

March 21, 2017

  • Revamped the corporate website and customer panel
  • Launched Ecom Fulfillment Services (EFS) with centres in Bengaluru, Lucknow, Kolkata and Delhi
  • Extended full-state coverage to Punjab, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Bihar and West Bengal. We attained capability of reaching around 60 percent of India’s population
2018

2018

March 13, 2018

  • Launched operations in Kerala
  • Opened Fulfilment Centres (FCs) in Kerala, Assam, Punjab and Bihar taking it to a total of 16 FCs.
  • Extended full-state coverage to Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Kerala, Assam, Odisha, Jharkhand and Chhattisgarh – taking full-state coverage to 20 states. We now have a capability of reaching around 95 percent of India’s population
  • Increased team size to over 17000 staff with presence in near 2400 cities/towns and 2500+ Delivery Centres
