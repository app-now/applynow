Logistics Parks

Agility Logistics Parks is a leading owner and operator of logistics and industrial parks across the Middle East, Indian subcontinent and Africa. We own and manage 21 million square meters of industrial land across 16 countries.

Located in prime industrial locations near critical transport hubs, Agility brings world-class infrastructure to emerging markets. Agility offers customers both build-to-suit facilities and ready-built warehouses, in addition to open storage and truck yards. Agility’s customer base spans the retail, pharmaceuticals, electronics, consumer packaged goods, transportation, chemicals, light manufacturing, oil and gas, and public sectors.
