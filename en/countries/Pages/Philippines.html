 Agility Philippines

With Agility, customers can turn their complex logistics issues into a cohesive, efficient supply chain. Our end-to-end solutions--from customs clearance and documentation processing to warehousing and trans-shipments--result in significant time and cost savings. Vendor-managed inventory systems pioneered by Agility are widely used by technology companies, and virtually all Philippine islands have access to our extensive domestic sea and air distribution network. We provide value-added services such as kitting, bundling and repackaging at our warehouses that offer over 22,325sqm of storage. Our specialties include project logistics to support large-scale industrial projects and a fairs and events team that supports exhibitions in major cities.
