CASE STUDIES

Meeting a major automaker’s open yard storage needs

Agility Jordan has converted more than 50,000 square meters of open yard storage situated in our facility in Aqaba Special Economic Zone into a local and regional distribution hub for one of the loading Korean automotive manufacturers. Agility helps the customer to reduce the lead time needed to respond to urgent sales leads in Jordan, Iraq and some countries located in the GCC. Agility also offers convenient, cost efficient and secure storage areas to fulfill the customer’s requirements.

Upgrading a telecom provider’s infrastructure on a tight timeline

One of the leading telecommunications solutions providers was awarded a project by three of the mobile telecommunications operators in Jordan to upgrade their 2G/3G infrastructure into 4G within a tight timeframe. Agility Jordan partnered with the provider by offering them a wide range of services, including customs clearance, in-land transportation, warehousing, local distribution, packing/repacking and reverse logistics. Additionally, Agility had assigned multiple warehouses in Amman to receive or dispatch equipment within a limited timeframe to meet an aggressive site installation schedule for multiple in-parallel projects. Agility fulfilled our customers’ complex needs and was awarded ‘Best Logistics Provider of the Year’ by the customer during their annual awards event.

Dedicated garments warehouse helps apparel manufacturer save on labor and storage

Agility Jordan serves one of the region’s multinational apparel manufacturers by offering a dedicated garments warehouse to store and consolidate shipments, as well as conduct value-added services such as scanning, labeling and packing before the shipments are assigned to their final destination. Our customer runs three manufacturing units in Jordan and utilizes Agility’s warehouse to focus on their core manufacturing activities. This saves high capital investment and reduces labor and storage costs.
