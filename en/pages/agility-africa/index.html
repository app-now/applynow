Community Investments

Agility is bridging the skills gap by putting training, education and development at the heart of its Africa strategy. At its warehouse park in Tema, Ghana, Agility has partnered with Oiada International, a local NGO, to provide free training to young adults. The program offers education in computer and workplace skills, brick making and training as an electrician’s apprentice.
