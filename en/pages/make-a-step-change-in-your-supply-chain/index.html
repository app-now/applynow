Introduction

Agility is one of the world’s leading providers of integrated logistics. With our extensive experience and resources our expert team are able to customise supply chain solutions for your specific business needs. Using a proven and scalable process and drawing from our large network of people, we can utilise our logistical expertise to create solutions that accommodate the most dynamic and category-specific markets.

As more and more pharmaceutical manufacturers look to bridge the gap between their supply chain efficiency and the demands of the fast-paced pharmaceutical industry, they turn to Agility for help.

Whether you require better visibility, costs savings, an inventory management solution, or an entirely customised approach, we provide the right process, equipment and tools as well as the personal attention to manage your supply chain efficiently and cost-effectively.

The Agility Supply Chain Reviews

What is it?

What is the cost?

A consultative, bespoke process that will reduce the cost and increase the productivity of your international supply chain. We do this by reviewing, evaluating and optimising your supply chain and supporting processes.

This service is free. We are confident that following the Supply Chain Review you will want to follow our implementation plan to deliver the benefits of our findings.

What are the benefits?

How many staff will be involved?

It will save you money. These savings take the form of eliminating non value add activities from your business process and reduced transport and distribution costs through increased consolidation and inventory visibility. We also look at strategies to increase stock turn and improve service to your customers.

Our mantra is “go see and ask why”. We will conduct interviews with key members of the supply chain function and those who interact with it at all levels as well as collecting data. However we always allow staff to get on with their day jobs to ensure business as usual.

How long does it take?

Who does it?

Typically 2-3 days. However as each Supply Chain Review is a bespoke process we will flex this time according to the need and complexity of your supply chain. We hope to collect data and present back to you within 3-4 weeks.

The review is conducted by our Supply Chain Solutions Manager and supporting team. We apply LEAN methodologies and business process re-engineering, continuous improvement and cost down strategies.

How does it work?

Implementation

With you. We spend time on site, in your business to understand your how your international supply chain is managed, the processes and systems you use and the service your market demands.

Our Implementation manager is present from day one of the review, giving him an intimate understanding of your business and input into the strategy agreed. He is therefore ideally placed to guarantee a seamless transition.

Case Study

Customer Challenge

A leading international high street retailer of beauty and cosmetic products were experiencing issues in aligning increased output from their manufacturing site in the UK with increasing service demands from a variety of overseas retail outlets as they expanded their global footprint through a network of owned and franchised business models. In order to optimise this rapidly evolving and increasingly demanding supply chain the client engaged Agility to develop an agile, lean and demand driven operating model.
