Description

The ShipA Freight app makes it easy to get air and ocean freight quotes and track your shipments on the go. ShipA Freight is powered by Agility, a global freight forwarder offering air, ocean and road freight; warehousing and distribution; and specialized services in project logistics, chemical logistics and fairs & events. With our global network of logistics experts and industry-leading technology, we ensure that your goods arrive safely and reliably every time.
